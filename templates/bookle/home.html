Welcome to Bookle!

To play the game, start entering the name of a novel then select it from the drop down menu. If any of the details of your guessed novel match the answer, those details will go green. If the release year is within the five years of the answre or number of words in the title if within 2 words of the answer, these will go yellow - the arrows are there to help too! There is a daily puzzle but you can also play the past most difficult and popular puzzles if one isn't enough!
